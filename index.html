About Dr. Mujeeb

Your Trusted Family Physician

Dr. Mujeeb is a dedicated General Physician with over 15 years of experience caring for patients of all ages. He believes good medicine starts with truly listening — taking the time to understand each patient before recommending a clear, practical treatment plan.

From everyday illnesses to long-term conditions like diabetes and high blood pressure, his goal is simple: help you feel better and stay healthy, with honest advice you can trust.

  • MBBS, FCPS (Family Medicine) — fully licensed practitioner
  • 15+ years of clinical & primary-care experience
  • Special focus on diabetes & hypertension management
  • Patient-first, compassionate approach to every visit
