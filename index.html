About Dr. Mujeeb

Your Trusted General Physician

Dr. Mujeeb is a dedicated General Physician (MBBS), certified in Critical Care Management from Aga Khan University (AKU). He completed his MBBS from Mohi-ud-Din Islamic Medical College, Mirpur (Azad Kashmir), and his house job at DHQ Hospital, Faisalabad.

He has served as a Medical Officer in Faisalabad and currently works as a Medical Officer at District TB Hospital, Faisalabad. Throughout his career he has focused on ethical, compassionate and evidence-based care — building trust with every patient and helping them achieve better health through proper diagnosis, treatment and prevention.
